DOM-based XSS

DOM XSS happens entirely in the browser: JavaScript takes attacker-controlled input (here the URL fragment, location.hash) and writes it to a dangerous sink. Nothing reaches the server, so server-side escaping and even CSP for reflected content don't apply — the fix is choosing a safe sink.

Vulnerable sink — innerHTML
Safe sink — textContent
