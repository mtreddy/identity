XSS attack vs. defense

Try the same payload against the unprotected and protected endpoints. A good payload: <script>alert(document.domain)</script> or <img src=x onerror=alert(1)>

Reflected

  • /vuln/search — payload runs
  • /safe/search — payload shown as inert text (+ CSP)

Stored

  • /vuln/comments — post a comment with a script tag; it runs for every viewer
  • /safe/comments — comments are escaped (+ CSP)

DOM-based

  • /dom — sink comparison (browser-only)

Your session cookie is HttpOnly, so even a working XSS here can't read it via document.cookie.
